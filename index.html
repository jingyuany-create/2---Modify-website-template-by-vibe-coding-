Abstract

This website experiments with an impressionist editorial aesthetic: soft, painterly color gradients paired with precise, contemporary typography and layout. The goal is to feel like a digital art publication—calm, refined, but still expressive—rather than a generic template.

Color & Texture

The background uses a layered gradient inspired by impressionist palettes: warm light, muted blues, and soft greens. Instead of a flat solid color, the page suggests the feeling of a canvas wash. The content card floats on top as a crisp surface, creating tension between atmospheric color and structured information.

Typography & Layout

Titles use a serif display face to evoke editorial and gallery identities, while body text stays in a clean sans-serif for readability. Small all-caps labels and careful letter-spacing create a hierarchy similar to magazine layout. Generous white space and a single-column grid keep the composition quiet, so the color field can stay playful without feeling chaotic.
